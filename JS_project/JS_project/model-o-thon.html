What is Model-O-thon?

Junior scientist provides a platform for young talents to rediscover creativity with the most fundamental and efficient starting step-model making.

Know More

How the Event is Conducted

  1. Teams will be required to conceptualize their models in detail and deliver a presentation to the panel of judges in less than 5 minutes
  2. The judges will ask questions regarding the working and applications of your models to test the conceptual understanding of the matter.
  3. The team that stands out will win the competition!
  4. Use of charts and paperwork for presentation is advisable. All types of models (working and non-working) are acceptable and will be evaluated on the same grounds.
  5. For 5th to 7th: Applications of basic science in Everyday life. For 8th to 12th: Empower India's progress through innovative solutions.

Rules

  • 1. The participating team should consist of NOT more than 3 members
  • 2. Participants should be well-equipped with all essential materials.
  • 3. No hazardous materials are allowed.
  • 4. The time limit for the presentation is 10 minutes.
  • 5. All decisions by the judges are final.
